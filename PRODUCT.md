# Joker Manager Product Context

## Register

Product.

## Purpose

Joker Manager tracks Joker Jackpot tournament runs accurately and stores an auditable history in Google Sheets. The TV display promotes the next Joker tournament to players already inside PlayLive Melbourne.

## Users

Staff use the dashboard after late registration closes in CasinoWare. They enter final entry counts, submit the physical card draw result, and correct records when needed.

Players see only the TV display. It should sell the story that the jackpot is climbing, the deck is shrinking, and the next Joker event is worth entering.

## Principles

- Jackpot rules are calculated by the system, not typed by staff.
- Staff workflows must be fast, clear, and low-risk.
- Critical actions require stronger confirmation.
- Sensitive corrections require the shared staff password and change logging.
- The TV route is promotional, not operational.
- Google Sheets is the audit database for version 1.
