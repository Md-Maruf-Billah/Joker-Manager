# Joker Manager Design Context

## Staff UI

The staff UI should feel like a calm control panel for a poker room floor:

- Dark charcoal and green-felt base.
- Gold primary action and jackpot emphasis.
- Purple and green Joker accents.
- Large form fields and buttons.
- No tiny operational controls.
- Minimal animation during data entry.

## TV Display

The TV display should feel premium, bold, and readable at distance:

- Jackpot amount is the largest element.
- Cards remaining is second priority.
- Probability appears only at 20 cards or fewer.
- Latest Joker winner appears only from 53 to 48 cards remaining.
- Motion is slow, legible, and tier-based.

## Color Strategy

Restrained product UI with dark tinted neutrals and gold as the primary accent. Purple, green, and red are reserved for Joker state, admin state, and danger state.
